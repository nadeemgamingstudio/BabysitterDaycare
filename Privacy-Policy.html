🍭 Privacy overview

Babysitter Daycare Mania is a delightful simulation where players take care of adorable babies and toddlers — feed them, play mini‑games, put them to sleep, and create a loving daycare. We respect your privacy as much as we care for little ones. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you stay in control.

Our promise: We never sell personal data, fully comply with child safety laws (COPPA / GDPR‑K), and ensure a safe digital daycare environment.

🍼 1. What data we collect

We only gather minimal information to keep the daycare running smoothly and improve your experience:

  • 📱 Device & technical info — model, OS version, language, country, advertising identifier (AAID / IDFA), IP address. Used for analytics & ad functionality.
  • 🎮 Game progress & preferences — babies unlocked, items collected, level progression, sound/vibration settings. Stored locally on your device (no forced account).
  • 📢 Ad interaction data — via Google AdMob. AdMob may use identifiers to serve contextual or personalized ads (respecting child settings).
  • 🍼 In-app purchases — if you buy special outfits or remove ads, Google Play Billing processes the payment. We never see your card details.

We never ask for real names, contacts, precise location, or access to your media files.

🍼 2. How we use your data

Your information helps us provide a safe and joyful babysitting experience:

  • Save game progress — remember your daycare stats, unlocked items, and daily rewards.
  • Analyze & improve performance — using Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics to fix bugs and optimize gameplay.
  • Show advertisements (optional rewarded videos for extra coins) via Google AdMob.
  • Verify in-app purchases and grant virtual goods.
We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

🍼 3. Third‑party services (SDKs)

To deliver core features, we rely on trusted partners:

  • Google AdMob – advertising network. May collect device data and IP for relevant ads. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – aggregated usage data and crash reports. Helps us keep Babysitter Daycare Mania stable and fun.
  • Google Play Billing – secure handling of in-app purchases.

All partners comply with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and other applicable regulations.

🍼 4. Data retention & deletion

Your daycare progress stays on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove local progress. No account is required. To request deletion of any data linked to your advertising ID or analytics, email us at nadeemgamer180@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

You can reset your advertising ID via device settings (Android: Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID).

🍼 5. Your ad choices & opt‑out

We give you control over personalized ads:

  • On Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • For users under the age of consent (region‑specific), only non-personalized contextual ads are served (COPPA mode).
  • Rewarded video ads are always voluntary — watch them for in‑game bonuses only if you choose.
Child‑safe design enabled & COPPA compliant

🍼 6. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR‑K)

Babysitter Daycare Mania is designed for families and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA).

  • No personal data (name, email, address) is required to enjoy the game.
  • If the Play Store identifies a child account, personalized ads and certain analytics are automatically disabled.
  • Parents or guardians: if you believe your child has provided any personal data (e.g., by contacting support), please email us and we will delete it immediately.

🍼 7. Data security

We implement industry best practices: encrypted connections (TLS), limited data retention, and strict access controls. Although no system is 100% immune, we continuously update our security measures to protect your privacy.

🍼 8. Your privacy rights

Depending on your location (EEA, UK, California, Brazil, India), you may have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold (via advertising ID correlation).
  • Request correction or deletion of data.
  • Opt-out of the "sale" of personal data (we never sell).
  • Withdraw consent for analytics by resetting your advertising ID.

To exercise any right, email nadeemgamer180@gmail.com with subject “Privacy Request – Babysitter Daycare Mania”. We will reply within a reasonable timeframe.

🍼 9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. If significant changes occur, we will notify you via an in-game notice or update the effective date on this page. Continued use of Babysitter Daycare Mania means you accept the revised policy.
